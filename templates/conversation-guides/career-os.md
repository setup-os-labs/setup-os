# Career OS Conversation Guide

Use this when planning a local career, job search, or professional growth assistant.

## Goal

What career outcome should the system support?

## Inputs

What can it read: resume, LinkedIn export, portfolio, writing samples, job posts, company lists, or practice notes?

## Strategy

What roles, companies, compensation targets, locations, and constraints matter?

## Workflows

Should it track applications, tailor resumes, draft outreach, prepare for conversations, or maintain a learning plan?

## Approval Rules

What messages, applications, or profile edits require explicit approval?

## Existing Tools To Check

Ask whether ATS trackers, resume tools, CRM tools, or job boards can be composed.
