# Health OS Conversation Guide

Use this when planning a local health, wellness, or care coordination assistant.

## Goal

What health or wellness outcome should the system support?

## Constraints

List medical constraints, clinician instructions, allergies, injuries, and personal boundaries.

## Inputs

What can it read: notes, lab results, wearable exports, medication lists, diet logs, exercise logs, sleep data, or appointment history?

## Workflows

Should it track reminders, habits, questions for doctors, lab trends, exercise, sleep, diet, or care plans?

## Alerts

What reminders or risk signals should it send?

## Approval Rules

What must remain human-approved or clinician-approved?

## Existing Tools To Check

Ask whether existing calendars, wearable exports, note systems, or health record portals can be composed.
